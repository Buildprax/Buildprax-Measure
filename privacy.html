Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

1. Introduction

BuildPrax ("we", "our", or "us") operates the BUILDPRAX MEASURE PRO application and website (collectively, the "Service"). This Privacy Policy informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service.

2. Data Collection

BUILDPRAX MEASURE PRO is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal personal information:

  • Registration Information: When you download the free trial, we collect your name, email address, company name, phone number, and address (optional) to provide you with the download link and welcome email.
  • Payment Information: All subscription payments are processed securely through PayPal. We do not store credit card information. PayPal handles all payment data according to their privacy policy and PCI-DSS compliance standards.
  • App Usage Data: The application stores your projects and measurements locally on your device. This data is not transmitted to our servers unless you explicitly export or share it.

Device Identification and Licensing

Buildprax Measure Pro uses a locally generated device identifier (hardware fingerprint) for licensing and trial management purposes.

This identifier is created and stored on the user's device. The device identifier is generated and used locally and is not transmitted to our servers. It is used only to:

  • Manage trial periods
  • Validate subscription licenses

No drawings, project data, or measurement information are transmitted to external servers.

The application does not collect personal data for advertising, profiling, or analytics purposes.

Project files, drawings, and measurements remain on the user's device and are not uploaded by Buildprax Measure Pro.

For questions regarding privacy or licensing, please contact: support@buildprax.com

3. Data Storage

All project data, measurements, and drawings are stored locally on your device. We do not have access to your project files or measurement data unless you explicitly share them with us for support purposes.

4. Data Usage

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain the Service
  • Send you welcome emails and product updates
  • Process subscription payments through PayPal
  • Respond to support requests
  • Improve our Service

5. Third-Party Services

Our Service uses the following third-party services that may collect information:

  • PayPal: Processes all subscription payments. Please review PayPal's privacy policy at paypal.com.
  • Microsoft Store: For Windows users, the app is distributed through Microsoft Store. Microsoft may collect usage data according to their privacy policy.
  • Google Analytics: Our website uses Google Analytics to understand website traffic. This data is anonymized and aggregated.

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of marketing communications

To exercise these rights, please contact us at support@buildprax.com.

8. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • Email: support@buildprax.com
  • Website: www.buildprax.com
