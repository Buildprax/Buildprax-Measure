Features for Construction Professionals

📐 Manual PDF Measurement

Measure lengths, areas, and counts directly on your PDF drawings with precision.

📊 Export to Excel

Export all results of each module to Excel.

🧱 Bills of Quantities

Create accurate and professional looking bills of quantities linked to drawings.

📉 Tender Comparisons

View tenders received per item next to each other and identify the lowest rates.

🧾 Monthly Certificates

Create monthly certificates linked to updated bills of quantities with ease.

📘 Estimates

Prepare estimates with quantities and values linked to drawings and bills of quantities.

💸 Cashflow Forecasts

Create cashflow forecasts with the press of a button.

📈 Feasibilities

Professional feasibilities linked to drawings and/or B of Q costs, including financial calculations, escalation, income, expenditure and return.
